About us

Welcome to our Madra Maith - Dog walking! We know how much you love your furry friend, and we're here to help give them the exercise and attention they need while you're away. Our team of experienced and reliable dog walkers are dedicated to providing top-notch care for your pup. Our team is fully insured and we are committed to the safety and well-being of all the dogs in our care. We understand that your dog is a member of your family and we will treat them as such.

Services

We offer a variety of walking services to suit your needs, including solo walks, group walks, and jogs. We also offer dog sitting and doggy daycare services for those times when you need a little extra help.

Booking

We offer flexible scheduling and competitive pricing, and we serve North county Dublin. Contact us today to learn more and book your dog's first walk!
